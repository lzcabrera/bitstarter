FAQ

What to expect from a coaching session?

In each session, our coaches will:

  • give you tips on how to improve your headshot and resume
  • comment on whether the song or monologue selection was a good choice for you
  • offer specific performance feedback
  • address whether your hair, makeup and attire works for an audition
  • highlight what you should work on for next time
  • answer one question of your choice
Why this project?

We created Stagelighter because we believe that getting solid advice from those who've succeeded in the industry is one of the best ways to move a career forward.

'Talent Searches' and flashy slogans like 'Become a Star' just are not our style. We know the sacrifices required to pursue a career in theatre - we have been there ourselves.

Stagelighter will bridge the gap, we will let anyone with Broadway goals connect with the best professionals in the industry and avoid the pitfalls of going at it alone.

What is the money going to be used for?

The money raised will be used to port the application over to newer technologies so we can improve user experience and performance by having @lauracabrera and @pdub23 working on it full-time for 6-months.
